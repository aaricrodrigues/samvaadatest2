About Us

Founded in 2022, our club has had only one mission at it's core; to develop and establish a beautiful digital prowess for our college. We aim to bring out and help grow the skills and talents of students under our club's domain alongside showcasing the student life and talents of NMAMIT on various social media platforms. The club operates with 3 main fields; Media, Content and Documentation. Our media team covers all sorts of events while our content team tirelessly brings out new content to keep our student audience engaged. The documentation team handles all event documentation and reporting as well as skillfully managing agendas.
